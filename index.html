Based out of San Francisco, Sutro is a hardware startup developing a smart pool monitor that measures water chemistry and delivers the appropriate chemicals as they're depleted. CodeBase is working with Sutro to develop a cross-platform mobile application that allows pool boys to plan and track their route for the day, send status reports and statistics to their customers, and check billing information.
